You have an amazing brain!
Get curious and learn about it!

Your brain is encased in complete darkness inside your skull. Your sensory oragans, your eyes, ears, nose and entire body feed it signals and then your brain interprets those so that you can experience life! How does it do this? What are neurons? What are the effects of damage to the brain in different areas? How does the brain adapt itself? Why do we have conflicting thoughts and behaviors? Well, we can learn about this stuff!!
