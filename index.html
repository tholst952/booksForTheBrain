You have an amazing brain!
Get curious and learn about it!

Your brain is encased in complete darkness inside your skull. The sensory oragans (eyes, ears, nose, hands etc.) send it signals and then your brain interprets those so that you can experience life! How does it do this? What are neurons? How does the nervous system work? How does the brain adapt itself over time? Why do we have conflicting thoughts and behaviors? Well, we can learn about this stuff!!

FOOD FOR THOUGHT

Incognito: The Secret Lives of the Brain

David Eagleman

If the conscious mind--the part you consider to be you--is just the tip of the iceberg, what is the rest doing? One of the key themes of Incognito is the idea that much of what goes on in the brain happens outside of our conscious awareness.

Get the Book

Livewired: The Inside Story of the Ever-Changing Brain

David Eagleman

The magic of the brain is not found in the parts it's made of but the way those parts reweave themselves in an electric, living fabric. In Livewired, you will learn how the brain is constantly rewiring itself.

Get the Book

Behave: The Biology of Humans at Our Best and Worst

Robert M. Sapolsky

From the celebrated neurobiologist and primatologist, a landmark examination of human behavior, both good and bad, and a answer to the question: Why do we do the things we do?

Get the Book

Determined: The Science of Life Without Free Will

Robert M. Sapolsky

Determined offers a synthesis of what we know about how consciousness works—the tight weave between reason and emotion and between stimulus and response in the moment and over a life.

Get the Book
